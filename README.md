# Musi Riverfront Development — AI-Powered Planning
### 44 km Infrastructure Corridor | Telangana Government Project

🏛️ **Client:** Telangana Technological Services (Govt. of Telangana)  
📅 **Duration:** June–August 2024  
🎤 **Presented at:** Global AI Summit 2024  
👁️ **Reviewed by:** Senior Government Officials  

---

## Project Summary

Worked as a Design Engineer on the 44 km Musi River infrastructure and riverfront planning project, one of the landmark urban redevelopment initiatives of the Telangana Government.

---

## Contributions

- Traced the entire Musi River alignment using **CAD Mapper** and **AutoCAD**
- Developed **2D engineering models** for planning and alignment verification
- Created **3D visualization models** using **SketchUp**
- Supported development of a **physical 3D printed scale model**
- Interpreted alignment layouts and verified dimensional accuracy
- Performed CAD-based quantity understanding for infrastructure scope

---

## AI & Smart Infrastructure Proposals

- AI-based river health monitoring systems
- Drone-based surveying and monitoring framework
- Water quality IoT monitoring network
- VR simulation concepts for public engagement and stakeholder presentations
